ARROWHEAD PLANTS

Scientific Name

  • Syngonium podophyllum

What is Arrowhead Plant?

  • Arrowhead plant (Syngonium podophyllum) is a beautiful foliage plant, one of the most popular species of the Araceae family. Due to its air-purifying qualities and good looks, arrowhead plant is often cultivated as a houseplant. Every part of this plant is toxic, so it's best to keep it away from kids and pets.

History of Arrowhead Plant

  • The discovery of the Arrowhead plant can be traced back to the 1800s when European explorers first began exploring the rainforests of Central and South America. Along with many other exotic plant species, the Arrowhead plant was brought back to Europe as a botanical curiosity.

Benefits of Arrowhead Plant

  • The arrowhead plant has beautiful, elegant leaves and is used for decorating indoor environments. It helps absorb formaldehyde, ammonia, and other toxic gases in the air, improving indoor air quality.
