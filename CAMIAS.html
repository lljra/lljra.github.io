CAMIAS

Scientific Name

  • Averrhoa bilimbi

What is Camias?

  • Also known as bilimbi or cucumber tree, Camias is a tropical fruit from the same family as starfruit. Its small, cucumber-shaped fruits grow directly on the trunk and branches and are known for their very sour taste.

History of Camias

  • Native to Malaysia and Indonesia, Camias is called belimbing asam in Malay. Its scientific name honors the philosopher and physician Averroes, and the name “bilimbi” comes from the Malay word belimbing.

Benefits of Camias

  • Vitamin C-rich: Supports immune health and skin clarity.
    Traditional remedy: Used for coughs, colds, and blood pressure.
    Anti-inflammatory: Helps treat swelling and skin conditions.
    Low-calorie: Intense flavor with minimal calories.
