HEART OF JESUS

Scientific Name

  • Caladium bicolor

What is Heart of Jesus?

  • Heart of Jesus is a stunning tropical perennial known for its thin, heart-shaped, and translucent leaves. Its scientific name is Caladium bicolor, and it is popular as both a garden plant and a houseplant. The foliage is admired for its vibrant blends of pink, red, white, and green, often highlighted by striking veins and borders.

History of Heart of Jesus

  • Originated in the tropical rainforests of South and Central America, particularly Brazil. Introduced to Europe in the late 1700s, it became highly popular as a greenhouse ornamental. Today, hybrid varieties are cultivated worldwide for their unique colors and patterns.

Benefits of Heart of Jesus

  • This plant is widely used as a shade-loving ornamental, adding vivid color to gardens, patios, and indoor spaces. It enhances aesthetic appeal and creates a tropical atmosphere.
