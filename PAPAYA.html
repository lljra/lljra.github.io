PAPAYA

Scientific Name

  • Carica papaya

What is Papaya?

  • Papaya is the succulent fruit of a large plant from the family Caricaceae. It is cultivated across the tropical world and into warm subtropical regions. The fruit is slightly sweet with a pleasant musky tang and is widely used fresh or cooked.

History of Papaya

  • Papaya has long been cultivated in Mexico and Central America and later spread globally with tropical agriculture.

Benefits of Papaya

  • Papaya contains papain and chymopapain, enzymes that aid digestion, tenderize meat, and have medical and industrial uses.
