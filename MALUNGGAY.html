MALUNGGAY

Scientific Name

  • Moringa oleifera Lam

What is Malunggay?

  • Moringa is a short-lived, fast-growing, drought-resistant, deciduous tree belonging to the family Moringaceae. It is often called the "miracle tree" or "tree of life" because almost all its parts are used for food and traditional medicine.

History of Malunggay

  • Moringa oleifera is believed to be native to the sub-Himalayan regions of northern India and was used in Ayurvedic medicine as early as 2000 BC. It later spread to Africa, Southeast Asia, and the Philippines.

Benefits of Malunggay

  • Malunggay leaves are rich in vitamins, minerals, and antioxidants. They support immunity, reduce inflammation, improve nutrition, and are widely used in traditional and modern medicine.
