GARDEN CROTON

Scientific Name

  • Codiaeum variegatum

What is Garden Croton?

  • Garden Croton is a colorful ornamental plant known for its bright, multicolored leaves. Its scientific name is Codiaeum variegatum, and it’s popular in gardens and as a houseplant because the foliage can show shades of red, yellow, orange, green, and purple.

History of Garden Croton

  • Garden Croton originated in Southeast Asia and the Pacific Islands. It became popular worldwide in the 19th century due to its colorful leaves and ornamental value.

Benefits of Garden Croton

  • In subtropical and tropical climates, Garden Croton is frequently used as landscape shrubs for dramatic hedges, bold focal points in gardens, and potted specimens around buildings. It enhances the beauty of gardens, homes, schools, and public spaces. It is easy to grow in warm, sunny climates and may also help improve air quality, making it a popular ornamental plant.
